Core Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

A focused toolkit built specifically for the UiTM FYP lifecycle — from the very first draft to the final approved chapter.

Rich Chapter Drafting

A full-featured rich text editor with formatting toolbar, word counter, and real-time draft saving. Draft, revise, and resubmit any of the 11 FYP chapter sections with contextual academic guidelines embedded right beside your workspace.

Built-in Academic Guidelines

AI Academic Checker

Powered by Google Gemini, the AI auditor scans your draft for grammar issues and evaluates against chapter-specific checklists — generating a structured report instantly.

Supervisor Review Flow

Supervisors receive instant email notifications on submission. A dedicated full-screen evaluation panel lets them read the draft, tick a rubric checklist, write feedback, and record a decision — all in one place.

Consultation Logs

Students log consultation sessions with date, time, platform, duration, and agenda. Supervisors approve via email or the portal, building a transparent, verified timeline toward the 7-session requirement.

Google Doc Export

Compile any combination of approved chapters into a single, professionally structured Google Doc — automatically saved to Drive and ready for final refinement or institutional submission.

Admin Control Centre

Complete system oversight — manage users, supervisors, programs, and monitor every submission and consultation log across the entire faculty cohort with real-time analytics.

How It Works

From registration
to graduation.

A guided, structured process that keeps every stakeholder informed and every deadline on track.

01

Register & Select Supervisor

Students complete their academic profile, select a registered supervisor, and submit a registration request. Supervisors approve or reject directly in the portal — the student is notified by email.

02

Draft Each Chapter Section

Write chapter sections in the rich editor with live academic guidelines. Save drafts at any time — only submit when you're ready for supervisor review.

03

Supervisor Reviews & Feedback

Supervisors evaluate submissions against a structured rubric, write detailed feedback, and mark chapters as Approved or Needs Revision. Students are notified instantly.

04

Log Consultations & Build Your Record

Every meeting is documented and approved, building a verified consultation timeline toward the minimum 7-session requirement for FYP completion.

05

Compile & Export Final Document

Select approved chapters, set your document title, and export a polished Google Doc to Drive — ready for final submission to your faculty.
